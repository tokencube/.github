# Code of Conduct

The TokenCube organization adopts the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) as its Code of Conduct for every `tokencube/*` repository and community space.

Full text: <https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>

## Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to `conduct@zero.ski`. All reports are reviewed promptly and kept confidential.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces (repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions, chat, and events) and whenever an individual is representing the community in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Community leaders follow the enforcement ladder described in the Contributor Covenant 2.1 (correction, warning, temporary ban, permanent ban). Repositories may publish their own `CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md` to override this org-wide default, but may not relax the baseline.
